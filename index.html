Lost or Damaged Starlight Passport Policy

If an attendee loses or damages their Starlight Passportespecially if it affects the microchipthey may request a replacement under the following conditions:

Replacement Process:

  • Report the Loss or Damage: Attendees must contact Tropical Wave organizers via social media before the next event.
  • Proof of Identity: Proof of identity must be provided.
  • Damaged Passport: If the passport is damaged but still physically present, it must be submitted for assessment.

Verification:

  • Organizers will verify the attendees past attendance records.
  • If approved, a new passport will be issued with the same number of stamps.
  • The passport is embedded with a microchip that provides exclusive interactive features during events; please handle with care, as physical damage may result in limited access.

Replacement Fee:

A fee of 100300 (TBD) will be required to cover reprinting and chip reprogramming costs. This ensures only legitimate replacements are processed.

Final Disclaimer:

  • Each attendee is only allowed ONE replacement throughout the three-event series.
  • Lost or damaged passports cannot be transferred to another person once replaced.

About Us

Tropical Wave is a non-profit group of Asian-pop performers based in Negros Oriental. Were a community of local talents brought together by our love for Asian pop music and dance. Through fun events, exciting performances, and creative collaborations, we aim to connect with fellow fans, celebrate our shared interests, and keep the energy of Asian pop culture alive in our community.

Vision

A leading community of inclusive and passionate performers in Negros Oriental, united by the love for Asian pop culture, spreading joy and cultural appreciation through shared talents in dance and music.

Mission

Tropical Wave is a non-profit organization dedicated to discovering and nurturing local talents who share a passion for Asian pop. Through performances, events, and collaborative activities, we aim to create an inclusive space that celebrates diversity, strengthens community bonds, and keeps the spirit of Asian pop music and dance alive and thriving.

Our Core Values

We embrace diversity and provide a welcoming space for individuals of all backgrounds, abilities, and identities who share a love for Asian pop culture.

We are driven by our shared enthusiasm for dance and music, striving to express joy, creativity, and excellence in every performance.

We foster strong bonds among members and audiences alike, using our shared interests to build friendships, promote teamwork, and uplift one another.
