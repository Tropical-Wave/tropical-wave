Tropical Wave Passport

The Tropical Wave Passport is your ultimate event adventure tracker! Every event you attend earns you a stamp, bringing you closer to exclusive rewards.

“Collect Stamps. Earn Rewards. Make Memories.”

Rewards You Can Earn

Bronze Tier (3 Stamps)

Unlock exclusive Tropical Wave digital wallpapers and a special mention on our social media!

Silver Tier (6 Stamps)

Receive a limited edition Tropical Wave sticker pack and a 10% discount voucher for official merchandise.

Gold Tier (9 Stamps)

Get a free Tropical Wave keychain, priority access to one panel/workshop at the next event, and a shoutout during the closing ceremony!

Platinum Tier (12 Stamps)

Congratulations! You've earned a free pass to the next Tropical Wave event, an exclusive meet & greet opportunity (subject to availability), and a featured spot in our event highlights video!

Terms and Conditions

  • Stamps must be collected officially at designated event booths or activities.
  • Passport is non-transferable. Rewards are for the passport holder only.
  • Lost or stolen passports cannot be replaced. Keep it safe!
  • Reward redemption details will be provided at the event or on our website.
  • Tropical Wave reserves the right to modify rewards or terms with prior notice.
  • Stamps are valid only for the event edition they were issued for.

Passport FAQ

You'll receive your Tropical Wave Passport upon entry to the event when you present your ticket. Look for the Passport Issuance booth near the main entrance.

Typically, each designated activity or booth will offer one unique stamp. Some special challenges or workshops might offer bonus stamps. Check the event guide for details!

Stamps are generally awarded for participation or attendance at specific activities during the event. Unfortunately, stamps cannot be awarded retroactively for missed activities.
