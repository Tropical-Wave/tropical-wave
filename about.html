Vision

A leading community of inclusive and passionate performers in Negros Oriental, united by the love for Asian pop culture, spreading joy and cultural appreciation through shared talents in dance and music.

Mission

Tropical Wave is a non-profit organization dedicated to discovering and nurturing local talents who share a passion for Asian pop. Through performances, events, and collaborative activities, we aim to create an inclusive space that celebrates diversity, strengthens community bonds, and keeps the spirit of Asian pop music and dance alive and thriving.

Our Core Values

We embrace diversity and provide a welcoming space for individuals of all backgrounds, abilities, and identities who share a love for Asian pop culture.

We are driven by our shared enthusiasm for dance and music, striving to express joy, creativity, and excellence in every performance.

We foster strong bonds among members and audiences alike, using our shared interests to build friendships, promote teamwork, and uplift one another.
